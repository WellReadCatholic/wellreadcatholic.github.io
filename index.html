Check Out Some of Our Favorite Authors!

Sigrid Undset

Sigrid Undset (1882-1949) . Winner of the Nobel Prize of Literature in 1928, this Norwegian novelist is perhaps most well-known for her trilogy, Kristin Lavransdatter, set in Medieval Norway, but also published works, such as Ida Elisabeth, that depict modern life.

Hilaire Belloc

Hilaire Belloc (1870-1953) rose to literary fame for a series of satirical children's poems but was also a prolific author of travel, history, essays, and politics. While most literate Catholics are familiar with G.K. Chesterton, we have largely forgotten his equally worthy friend.

Josef Pieper

Josef Pieper (1904-1997) is perhaps best known for Leisure: The Basis of Culture, however his other works on virtue, aesthetics, and philosophy are all accessible and engaging.
